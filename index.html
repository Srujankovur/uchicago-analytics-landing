Analytics & Reporting Tools

Capturing the Potential of Data

The University of Chicago provides a comprehensive suite of business intelligence and reporting tools to help you gain insights, make data-driven decisions, and access institutional data. Whether you need interactive visualizations, enterprise reports, or custom analytics, our platforms support the full spectrum of analytical needs across the university.

Tableau

Interactive data visualization and business intelligence platform for creating dashboards and reports. Build dynamic visualizations and share insights across the university.

Access Tableau

SAP BusinessObjects

Enterprise reporting and analytics suite for structured business intelligence and operational reports. Access pre-built reports and financial data.

Access SAP BO

Oracle OBIEE

Comprehensive business intelligence platform for enterprise reporting, analysis, and dashboards. Query institutional data and create custom reports.

Access OBIEE

Orbit

Custom analytics and reporting solution for specialized data needs and institutional metrics. Purpose-built for UChicago's unique reporting requirements.

Access Orbit
